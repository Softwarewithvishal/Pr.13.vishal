About Our Institute

KD Polytechnic, Patan is a premier technical institute in North Gujarat, affiliated with the Gujarat Technological University (GTU). We offer diploma engineering programs in Computer Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Our mission is to provide industry-relevant, hands-on technical education that prepares students for real-world challenges.
